In the alpha phase, we develop and refine early versions of the product or service, exploring and testing possible language solutions across user journeys.

## What content designers do in alpha

Our content designers:

- design and write content for prototypes based on research and user feedback
- consider all routes that users take into the service, and content touchpoints across all journeys
- collaborate with the UCD and wider delivery team to refine design concepts based on user feedback
- make sure content is consistent with other NHS and GOV services
- make sure content across all journeys is consistent and simple to understand
- work with policy and subject matter experts to understand what information the service must collect or provide
- support user researchers’ preparation for user testing by identifying riskiest assumptions about language and information needs
- help shape information architecture and content patterns within the service
- iterate prototypes based on findings from user research
